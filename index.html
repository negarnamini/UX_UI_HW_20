Winter (Superfly) Ziplining
  • It's a scary yet thrilling experience
    With cutting-edge technology and side-by-side ziplines, you and a friend can fly through the BC backcountry in a rush of adrenaline, beauty, and speed. Superfly provides four unique ziplines that go from mountain to mountain to mountain to mountain.
  • Make sure to not eat before
    Eat a very light breakfast about an hour or so before you go ziplining. Some chopped fruit or even a bowl of cereal. You can pack lunch to eat after you've finished ziplining.
Skiing in Whistler
  • Leave as early as you can
    When you arrive for your reservation, make sure to get there as early as possible to ensure that you don’t miss the early skiing hours. It’s often good to arrive early in the morning as most people arrive one or two hours before noon.
  • Renting skis and snowboards are easy
    From beginner and high performance boards and skis to helmets and outerwear, you can choose your kit from the latest styles and technology in the industry. Rental shops are located conveniently close to hotels and lifts throughout Whistler Village, Upper Village and Creekside.
Whistler peak-to-peak 360° experience (Gondola)
  • Expensive, but you're on vacation
    Tickets for this experience cost around $80. But, a PEAK 2 PEAK Alpine Experience ticket allows you to visit Whistler and Blackcomb Mountains in one day, on the way receiving a bird's eye view of the forest and spectacular mountains, valleys and glaciers. A special silver glass-bottom gondola provides another unique perspective of the forest.
  • Purchase tickets when you get there
    The best way to purchase PEAK 2 PEAK tickets is to visit Whistler Blackcomb Guest Services or the Whistler Visitor Centre when you arrive in Whistler. Tickets for this experience do not sell out.
Glacier Sightseeing Experience
  • Breathtaking Panoramic views
    Hop into your flightseeing plane near Green Lake and take your seat. Then, watch as the land disappears beneath you and soar high over Whistler's rugged terrain. Admire panoramic views over the region's mighty glaciers, soaring peaks, shimmering lakes and dense forests.
  • Exciting 30min+ plane ride
    Take to the skies and enjoy breathtaking views over Canada's rugged mountainscape on this 30- or 40-minute glacier sightseeing experience by floatplane from Whistler. Listen as your pilot shares insight into the valleys' rich geography, and you can even upgrade your tour for a further 10 minutes' airtime.
